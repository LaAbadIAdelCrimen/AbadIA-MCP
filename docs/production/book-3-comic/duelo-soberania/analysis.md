# Analysis: Duelo de Soberanía

## Narrative Core
A tense intellectual confrontation between the Abbot (Authority) and Guillermo (Logic). The scene establishes the 'Virtuous Disobedience' and the 'Interrogatory Pattern'.

## Key Scenes
1. Entrance into the Refectory (Atmosphere).
2. The Abbot's vague command ("Clean this house of evil").
3. Guillermo's refusal and the metaphor of the Creator's logic.
4. The REASONS Canvas reveal on a linen napkin.
5. The Abbot's capitulation and specification.

## Visual Style
- **Cinematic Realistic**: Inspired by Jean-Jacques Annaud's 'The Name of the Rose' (1986).
- **Lighting**: Chiaroscuro, flickering candles, heavy shadows, dusty air.
- **Palette**: Muted earth tones, deep blacks, warm candle yellows, cold stone greys.
