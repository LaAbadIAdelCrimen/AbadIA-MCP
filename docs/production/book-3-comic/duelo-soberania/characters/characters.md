# Characters: Duelo de Soberanía

## Guillermo de Baskerville
- **Visual Reference**: Sean Connery in 'The Name of the Rose'.
- **Traits**: Aged but sharp, grey-bearded, piercing intelligent eyes, wearing a coarse brown Franciscan habit with a hood. Constant restless hands, sometimes holding lenses.
- **Role**: Master Investigator / Logical Orchestrator.

## Adso de Melk
- **Visual Reference**: Young Christian Slater.
- **Traits**: Late teens, wide-eyed, smooth face, tonsured hair, wearing a dark Benedictine novice habit. Looks on with a mix of awe and fear.
- **Role**: Support Agent / Pedagogical Adversary.

## El Abad
- **Visual Reference**: Michael Lonsdale (Abbot Abbone).
- **Traits**: Tall, authoritative, somber face hidden in shadows, wearing a rich, heavy black habit with ornate cross. Stern expression.
- **Role**: Governance / Stakeholder.
