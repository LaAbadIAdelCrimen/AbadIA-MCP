# ADR-005: The Chronicler (Dreamer) Identity

> **Status:** Accepted
> **Owner:** [[hermes-agent]]
> **Date:** 2026-05-15

## 1. Context
Session-to-session learning is required for the agent to evolve tactics (e.g., discovering that a certain monk always passes through the kitchen at Prime).

## 2. Decision
Establish "The Chronicler" (formerly "The Dreamer") as a distinct analytical persona responsible for post-mortem log refinery. The Chronicler extracts causal patterns and updates the Vault/Wiki.

## 3. Consequences
- "Ratcheting" effect: knowledge compounds across sessions.
- Clear separation between execution (Guillermo) and reflection (Chronicler).
