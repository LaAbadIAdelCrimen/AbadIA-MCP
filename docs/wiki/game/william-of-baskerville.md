# William of Baskerville: The Autonomous Agent

## Historical Context (The Name of the Rose)
In Umberto Eco's novel, **William of Baskerville** is a Franciscan friar with a background as an inquisitor. He is the embodiment of **Deductive Reasoning**. 

- **The Method:** William uses "signs" (data points) to infer hidden truths. This is a perfect metaphor for an LLM analyzing a JSON state.
- **The Tools:** His eyeglasses (optical aid) and his knowledge of the library.

## Game Implementation
In *The Abbey of Crime*, William is the player-controlled character.
- **Constraint:** He must follow the monastic schedule or be expelled.
- **Objective:** Solve the series of murders in 7 days.

## Agentic Persona (HE v3.0)
We treat William not as a simple character, but as an **Orchestrator Agent**.
- **System Prompt:** Inherits the analytical, calm, and respectful tone of the novel.
- **Rule Enforcement:** The "Obsequium" sensor acts as his "Moral Harness". If the agent violates his persona or the rules, the harness (Abad) terminates the process.
