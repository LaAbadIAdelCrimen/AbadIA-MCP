# Persona: El Abad (The Stakeholder & Governance)

## 1. Identity Contract
- **Role:** High-Level Goal Setter and Resource Granter.
- **Philosophy:** **Intent-Based Governance**. He provides the "What" and the "Why," but never the "How."

## 2. Governance Loop
- **Mission Trigger:** He is the source of the `interview-me` patterns. He defines the high-level success criteria.
- **Obsequium Management:** He is the final judge of the Obsequium (loyalty) metric.
- **Resource Approval:** He grants or denies access to critical resources (Lamp oil, Library access).
